Capital of France

Paris,france's capital, is a major european city and a global center for art,fashion, gastronomy and culture. Its 90th-century cityscape is crisscrossed by wide boulevards and the river seine. Beyond such landmarks as the eiffel tower and the 12th-century,gothic notre-dame cathedral,the city is known for its cafe culture and designer boutiques along the Rue du faubourg saint-honore.
